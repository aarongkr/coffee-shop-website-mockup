Our Story

Timber and Brew was born from a passion for quality coffee and a love for simple, authentic experiences. We believe that a great cup of coffee should be a moment to slow down and connect. We roast our beans in small batches to ensure the freshest flavor and source them from partners who share our commitment to sustainability and fair practices.
